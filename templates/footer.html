Faculty Member Help
Hi Faculty Members!
Thank you for using the TUP-C DIT Research Defense Web App. It seems like you need some help in order to use this Web Application.

For Department Head:
- You can Accept and Decline the Students research defense form requests.
- You can either upload your signature in this web app or do the traditional way of signing each research defense form
- Most of the Research Defense Form will go through you, and your response is required in order to process the form requests of every Students.

For Subject Teacher:
- You can Accept and Decline the Students research defense form requests.
- You can either upload your signature in this web app or do the traditional way of signing each research defense form
- You have the control for every Research Defense (Topic, Proposal, Final) of the Students. - You have the control to create Research Defense Schedule (Topic, Proposal, Final) for the Students.

For Panel / Advisers:
- You can Accept and Decline the Students research defense form requests.
- You can either upload your signature in this web app or do the traditional way of signing each research defense form


For Academic Affairs Office / Library / Research & Extensions
- You can Accept the Acknowledgement Receipt of the Students if he/she complies with the requirements for Research Subject.
- You can either upload your signature in this web app or do the traditional way of signing each research defense form
